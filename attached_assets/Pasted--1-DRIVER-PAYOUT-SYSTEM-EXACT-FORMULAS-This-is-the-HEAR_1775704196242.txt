💰 1. DRIVER PAYOUT SYSTEM (EXACT FORMULAS)

This is the HEART of your business. If this breaks → your company breaks.

🧠 Core Model (Simple but powerful)
Each order has:
Order Total (OT)
Delivery Fee (DF)
Platform Commission (PC)
Driver Earnings (DE)
Cash Collected (CC)
🔢 FORMULAS (LOCK THESE IN)
1. Platform Commission
PC = OT × 15% (or 10–20% range)
2. Driver Earnings
DE = DF + (Optional Tip)

👉 Example:

Delivery fee = 150 DOP
Tip = 50 DOP
Driver earns = 200 DOP
3. Cash Collected (if customer pays cash)
CC = OT + DF
4. Driver Wallet Balance
Driver Wallet = Total Earnings - Cash Collected Owed

👉 Example:

Driver collected: 5,000 DOP cash
Driver earnings: 1,500 DOP
Wallet = 1,500 - 5,000 = -3,500 DOP

👉 That means:
Driver owes YOU 3,500 DOP

🔐 PROTECTION RULES (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
✅ Rule 1: Cash Limit
Max Cash Holding = 10,000 DOP

→ If exceeded:
👉 Driver gets auto-locked

✅ Rule 2: Settlement System
Daily or weekly cash drop
Pay via:
Bank transfer
Agent pickup
Local office
✅ Rule 3: Trust Score
Late deliveries = penalty
Cancellations = penalty
High rating = bonus
💡 BONUS (THIS IS HOW YOU WIN DR)
Driver Gamification:
10 deliveries = +500 DOP bonus
Fast delivery bonus
Peak hour multiplier

👉 DR drivers hustle for incentives… not rules.
